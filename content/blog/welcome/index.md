We're excited to announce that **Pwnlentoni** is now the official CTF team of the **University of Padova!**

## Who We Are

We are a group of cybersecurity enthusiasts from the University of Padova who share a passion for Capture The Flag competitions. Our team brings together students from different departments, united by curiosity and the thrill of solving security challenges.

## What We Do

We participate in CTF competitions around the world, both online and on-site. We solve challenges in various categories, including:

- **Pwn**: Exploiting vulnerabilities in binary programs.
- **Reverse Engineering**: Analyzing compiled code to understand its functionality.
- **Web**: Finding and exploiting vulnerabilities in web applications.
- **Crypto**: Breaking cryptographic schemes and algorithms.
- **Forensics**: Analyzing digital artifacts to uncover hidden information.
- **Misc**: Challenges that don't fit into the above categories but require creative problem-solving.

If you're a student at **University of Padova** and you're interested in cybersecurity, come hang out with us!
